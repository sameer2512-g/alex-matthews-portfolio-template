Frequently Asked Questions

What's your design process?

I follow a structured approach: Discovery & Research → Strategy & Planning → Design & Prototyping → Testing & Refinement → Development Handoff. Each project is tailored to your specific needs and timeline.

How long does a typical project take?

Timeline varies by project scope. Landing pages typically take 2-3 weeks, full websites 4-8 weeks, and app designs 6-12 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! I work with clients worldwide and am experienced in remote collaboration. I'm flexible with time zones and use modern tools to ensure smooth communication throughout the project.

What's included in your design packages?

All packages include initial consultation, design concepts, revisions, final files, and basic support. Specific deliverables vary by package - I'll provide a detailed scope during our discussion.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes! I provide post-launch support and can help with updates, maintenance, and future enhancements. We can discuss ongoing support options based on your needs.

What if I need changes after the project?

Minor adjustments are included in the initial package. For larger changes or new features, I offer flexible hourly rates or can create a new project scope based on your requirements.
